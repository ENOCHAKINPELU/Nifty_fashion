Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How can I order? +
Simply browse our collection, add items to your cart, and checkout via WhatsApp. Our team will confirm your order details and arrange delivery. You can also reach out directly via WhatsApp or our social media channels to place an order.
Do you deliver nationwide? +
Yes! We deliver to all states in Nigeria. Expect 1-3 days delivery in Lagos and 2-5 days in other regions. Shipping fees vary depending on your location and will be calculated upon checkout.
What is your return policy? +
We accept returns within 2 days for unworn items with tags intact. Exchanges are also available for wrong sizes or defective items. Please contact us immediately if you have any issues with your order, and our customer service team will assist you.
What payment options do you offer? +
We offer flexible payment options including bank transfers and mobile payments. Pay on delivery is available in Lagos, while other areas require prepayment before shipping. All payment details will be provided during the checkout process.
How do I know what size to order? +
Each product listing includes a size guide. If you're unsure about sizing, please contact us via WhatsApp with your measurements, and we'll help you select the perfect fit. We offer sizes ranging from S to XXXL for most items.
